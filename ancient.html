The Migration Period

The Period of Migration is a term defining the chaotic period before and after the fall of the Western Roman Empire. Many migrations of peoples occured that drastically altered the demographics and cultures of Europe. Western Asian peoples moved into Eastern Europe and Germanic tribes marched westward and southward into former Roman territory.
